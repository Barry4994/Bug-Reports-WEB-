(ENGLISH VERSION)

DESCRIPTION: In the Basket there is an opportunity to increase the number of pairs of shoes by 8-digit or more.

Environment: Windows 10 (64 bit)
Browser: Google Chrome Version 103.0.5060.134 (Official Build), (64 bit)
TIME: 07/26/2022 (18:20 at Moscow time)

PLAYBACK STEPS:
1. Open https://surnin-nikitin.ru/
2. Add any product to the "CART"
3. Go to the shopping cart
4. In the quantity column, add a value of 2 or more

EXPECTED RESULT: The editor does not allow you to add more than 1 item of goods to 1 basket position.
THE ACTUAL RESULT: The editor allows you to add more than 10 units of goods to one basket position.
